Description

Gathering of various convenient facilities regarding the support of various locales (e.g. for the management of per-country bank holidays) and related character sets.

A locale as a BCP 47 plain string or binary, like "fr-FR" or <<"fr-FR">>, corresponding to "French (France)" here.
