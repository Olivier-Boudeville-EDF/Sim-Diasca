monitor_info()

monitor_info() = basic_utils:exit_reason() | noproc | noconnection

This information may be:

- the exit reason of the process

- or 'noproc' (process or port did not exist at the time of monitor creation)

- or 'noconnection' (no connection to the node where the monitored process resides)
